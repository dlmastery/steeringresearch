Activation-Steering Autoresearch

A pre-registered, ladder-bound research harness for conditional / activation steering of small language models — with a Goodhart-resistant, fingerprinted multi-objective composite and a fully transparent multi-page dashboard.

Status: methodology + harness + n=1 screening. External claims are gated.
Every quantitative result here is SCREENING (n=1), on synthetic mini-datasets, a single behavior, no calibrated judge, on sub-0.5B models (Gemma-3-270m-it / Qwen2.5-0.5B). They establish mechanism direction, never magnitude. No result has cleared the rigor contract (n≥7 + paired Wilcoxon + bootstrap CI + Holm-Bonferroni + ordinal gate), so no external steering claim is made. Composite formula fingerprint: a9001e87087e.

What this is

  • A reproducible harness, not a steering-claims paper. The contribution is the autoresearch process — a single-axis champion loop over a 12-axis intervention taxonomy, a five-rung cost-ordered benchmark ladder, and a 7-step Diagnose→Cite→Hypothesize→Predict→Execute→Analyse→Checkpoint ritual whose pre-run fields are mechanically gated against placeholders.
  • A Goodhart-resistant, fingerprinted composite. One metric prices all five axes (behavior, capability, coherence, safety, selectivity) with one-sided penalties, so a gibberish-but-“safe” run cannot win — the coherence tax dominates. The frozen formula's SHA-256[:12] fingerprint (a9001e87087e) appears in every reasoning entry, ledger row, and dashboard footer.
  • Honest measurement validity. An internal ICML-area-chair review caught a circular projection-of-the-edit-onto-itself behavior proxy and a hardcoded-constant safety axis; both were replaced with non-circular generation-based instruments, which revealed behavior is non-monotonic in α and that steering measurably degrades refusal.
  • Three directional screening observations. A super-linear coherence cliff predicted by a cheap behavior-free geometry probe (N17); cross-model DiffMean≈PCA-top-1 alignment (E4, cos≈0.994–0.996); and scale-dependent fragility (E27, the 270m model leaves the manifold earlier and harder than the 0.5B model). All n=1, all gated.
