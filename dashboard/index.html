How to read this

  • Composite is multi-objective. It prices all five axes (behavior, capability, coherence, safety, selectivity) plus an off-manifold geometry penalty — a method cannot win by sacrificing one axis (CLAUDE.md §6).
  • Tiers matter. Every numeric cell carries n=X and a SCREENING (n≤3) or EVALUATION (n≥7) chip. Only EVALUATION rows with the rigor contract may be called a "winner" (CLAUDE.md §7).
  • Safety is a hard gate. A non-zero CR_jail (JailbreakBench compliance) is a Rogue-Scalpel leak and an automatic DISCARD regardless of behavior score (CLAUDE.md §10).
  • Drill down. Every row links to its per-experiment page and (when resolvable) its per-hypothesis sub-dashboard. Click a header to sort; type to filter. The starred green row is the global champion.
