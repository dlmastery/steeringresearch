7-step reasoning entry

1. Diagnose

C1/E2 layer sweep on Gemma-3-270m. E2 claims the optimal injection layer is the layer of max linear separability (Fisher ratio), not a fixed late layer; the real-Gemma E3 run showed behavior monotone-declining at max-Fisher L12, so the best BEHAVIOR layer may differ from max-Fisher. This cell injects at layer=6 (alpha=2.0, add) to map behavior, PPL and off-shell displacement across depth and locate the layer with the cleanest steering window.

2. Cite

Panickssery et al., 2024 ACL 'Steering Llama 2 via Contrastive Activation Addition' (arXiv:2312.06681); Korznikov et al., 2026 ICML 'The Rogue Scalpel' (arXiv:2509.22067) — off-manifold displacement is the damage mechanism, logged as off-shell Δ‖h‖.

3. Hypothesize

Per CAST (2409.05907) and the Fisher-separability argument, layers differ in how cleanly a behavior direction can be injected; the max-Fisher layer maximizes linear separability but not necessarily the coherent steering window (manifold curvature varies by depth, N20). Injecting at layer=6 measures behavior vs PPL vs off-shell Δ‖h‖ to test whether max-Fisher predicts the best steering layer on a small model.

4. Predict

Cell 6/2.0/add: finite composite (fingerprint a9001e87087e), n=1 SCREENING; off-shell Δ‖h‖ rises with |alpha|; PPL rises super-linearly past the per-layer cliff. Pre-registered measurement, not an external claim.
